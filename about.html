About me

My name is Idris Adabanija, I was born in Osun State, Nigeria. I am from the western part of Nigeria (Yoruba). I was born into the family of 3, which am the second child.

I came to the United state in 2015, and i attended Harry S Truman College.

My college life was a bit challenging because I had difficulties adjusting to the educational system. I graduated from Truman College in 2018 with a GPA of 3.5, with High Honor.

While at Truman college, I joined Phi theta Kappa in 2017, and I received 3 Honor of Dean List and a presidential scholar.

I got admission to the Illinois Institute of Technology as a third year undergraduate to gain a bachelor of science in Information Technology Management.

To be able to achieve all I have now, I will not a hesitate mention my family that are so supportive. Eespecially my Dad who pays for my tuition.

They taught me about what life they have given me, how they want me to be educated and be a man that will be part of a progressive society.

Life have been kind of easy for me because i have families who i rely on to guide me, so i can be able to accomplish my set goals.

My set goal is to become a web designer and application developer, so i contribute to my country and the society at large.
